AppX Policy for Purchase of Office Chair by Employees 1. Purpose This policy outlines the guidelines for employees to purchase an office chair for work-related purposes, ensuring comfort, ergonomics, and productivity. 2. Eligibility 2.1. All full-time employees who require an office chair for their designated workspace at office are eligible for this policy. 3. Approval Process 3.1 Employees must submit a request to the HR department. 3.2 The request should include justification for the purchase and any specific ergonomic requirements. 3.3 The approval will be granted based on need and budget. 4. Budget & Reimbursement 4.1 The company will provide a predefined budget of 50% of Chair Value or Rs. 3000 whichever is lower for the purchase of an office chair. 4.2 If the cost exceeds the budget, the employee must cover the additional expense unless an exception is approved. 4.3 The Employee must purchase the Chair in the name of Organization (along with GST details) and must submit the proof of purchase (invoice/receipt) for reimbursement. 4.4 Reimbursement will be processed on or before 5th of subsequent month for the all submitted invoices. 5. Chair Specifications 5.1. Chairs must meet ergonomic standards, including adjustable height, lumbar support, and cushioned seating. 5.2. Chairs must be purchased from the Amazon / Flipkart/Chroma only. 6. Ownership & Maintenance 6.1 As the chair is purchased using company funds, it remains company property. 6.2 Employees must maintain the chair in good condition. 6.3 Upon resignation or termination, the employee may be required to return the chair, subject to company discretion. 7. Exceptions & Special Cases 7.1 Any exceptions to this policy require approval from management. 7.2 Special accommodations may be considered for employees with medical needs, subject to appropriate documentation.
