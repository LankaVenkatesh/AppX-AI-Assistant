 
 
 
AppX Joining Bonus Policy 
 
 
1. Purpose 
This policy aims to ensure transparency and consistency in administering the Joining Bonus as 
per company guidelines. 
 
2. Eligibility 
2.1. Employees who have been offered a Joining Bonus as part of their employment offer letter 
are covered under this policy. 
2.2. The Joining Bonus will be payable only after successful completion of the initial three (3) 
months from the employee’s date of joining. 
 
3. Payment Criteria 
3.1 The Joining Bonus will be disbursed upon the completion of three (3) months of continuous 
service from the date of joining. 
3.2 The employee must be actively employed with AppXcelerate at the time of disbursement. 
 
4. Deferment 
4.1 If an employee is placed under a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) and removed from a 
project due to performance issues, the disbursement of the Joining Bonus will be deferred  
until the successful completion o f the PIP process or until the employee is deployed/ 
redeployed to a project, whichever occurs earlier.  
 
5. Recovery Clause 
5.1. If the employment agreement is terminated by either party within one (1) year from the date 
of joining, the employee shall be required to refund the entire Joining Bonus amount  
received. 
5.2. The repayment must be completed within thirty (30) days from the employee’s last working 
day. 
5.3. All relevant documents such as the experience letter, relieving letter, and final settlement 
will be released only after clearance of such dues. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
6. Misconduct or Disciplinary Cases 
6.1 In cases involving misconduct, violation of company policies, or disciplinary action, the Joining 
Bonus may be withheld or cancelled entirely at the Company’s discretion. 
 
AppXcelerate reserves the right to amend, modify, or withdraw this policy at its discretion, with or 
without prior notice.
