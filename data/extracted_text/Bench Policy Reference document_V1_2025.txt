 
 
 
 
Bench Management & Transition Policy 
 
Company Name: AppXcelerate Solutions Pvt Ltd 
 
1. Purpose 
• The purpose of this policy is to ensure effective management of employees who are not currently 
assigned to billable client projects (“bench resources”) and to define clear procedures for 
redeployment, upskilling, and, if necessary, release from the organization. 
• This policy aims to balance business needs with employee welfare while ensuring transparency, 
fairness, and compliance with applicable laws. 
2. Scope 
This policy applies to: 
• All full-time employees of Appxcelerate Solutions Pvt Ltd in delivery or technical roles. 
• All employees who are without an active project assignment for a continuous period exceeding the 
bench duration limit. 
3. Definitions 
• Bench – A period during which an employee is not deployed on a revenue -generating or billable 
project but remains employed. 
• Bench Duration Limit – The maximum number of calendar days an employee may remain on the 
bench before a transition decision is made. Default limit: 45 days (may vary by business unit). 
• Resource Management Group (RMG) – The internal team responsible for allocation, redeployment, 
and bench tracking. 
4. Bench Period Management 
• Initiation of Bench Period – The bench period begins the day after an employee is released from their 
last billable project. 
• Duration – The standard bench duration is 45 days, extendable up to 60 days  in special cases with 
Business Unit Head approval. 
• Monitoring – RMG will maintain an updated bench list, reviewed weekly with Delivery Managers and 
HR. 
• Employees moved to the bench due to performance concerns will be placed on a Performance 
Improvement Plan (PIP) for a duration of one month. If performance does not meet the required 
standards during this period, the employment agreement will be terminated  immediately upon 
completion of the PIP. 
5. Redeployment Process 
During the bench period, the following steps will be taken to redeploy the employee: 
• Skill Matching – Evaluate employee’s skills against current and upcoming project requirements. 
• Cross-Skilling/Upskilling – Offer targeted learning programs aligned with in-demand skills. 
• Shadow Assignments – Assign to shadow roles or non-billable internal initiatives. 
 
 
 
 
 
• Temporary Assignments – Utilize in proposal support, quality reviews, testing, or other billable -
preparatory tasks. 
• Project Matching Communication – Employees must respond promptly to potential opportunities 
proposed by RMG. 
6. Communication & Review 
• Day 1 of Bench – Formal communication from RMG notifying the start of the bench period and 
available support/resources. 
• Mid-Period Review (Day 20) – Progress check involving Delivery Manager, RMG, and HR to assess 
redeployment status. 
• Final Review (Day 45) – If no assignment is confirmed, a transition plan is prepared, which may include 
release. 
7. Release Decision Criteria 
An employee may be released if: 
• The bench duration exceeds the defined limit without confirmed project assignment. 
• No suitable project opportunities are available despite redeployment efforts. 
• Required skills are not aligned to current or foreseeable business needs. 
• The employee declines reasonable project opportunities without valid reasons. 
8. Transition & Exit Process 
• Notice Period – Follow terms of the employment contract (e.g., 30–60 days). 
• Final Settlement – Clearances, handover, and settlement to be completed per HR & Finance policies. 
• Rehire Consideration – Employees may be considered for rehire if future skills match business 
requirements. 
• Outplacement Support – HR may provide limited job referral assistance. 
9. Compliance & Documentation 
• All redeployment attempts, communications, and decisions must be documented in the HR system 
for audit purposes. 
• This policy will be implemented in accordance with applicable labor laws, statutory requirements, 
and internal employment terms. 
 10. Ethical Standards  
• All actions under this policy will be handled with professionalism, fairness, and respect. 
• Decisions will not be influenced by age, gender, religion, disability, or any protected characteristic. 
 
Policy Owner: HR Operations – Appxcelerate Solutions Pvt Ltd
