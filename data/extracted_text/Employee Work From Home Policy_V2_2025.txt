 
 
  
APPX WORK FROM HOME (WFH) POLICY  
 
Effective Date: 1 Jan 2025 
Last Updated: 30 Dec 2024 
 
OBJECTIVE  
 
AppXcelerate Solutions Pvt Ltd ("AppX") recognizes the importance of flexibility in fostering employee 
productivity and work-life balance. This Work From Home (WFH) policy outlines the guidelines and 
procedures for employees to effectively utilize WFH arrangements, ensuring alignment with 
organizational goals and maintaining professional standards. 
ANNUAL WFH ALLOCATION:  
 
• Male Employees: 
o Eligible for 12 WFH days annually, credited at the start of the year. 
o Mid-year joiners receive WFH days on a pro-rata basis. 
• Female Employees: 
o Eligible for 18 WFH days annually, credited at the start of the year. 
o Mid-year joiners receive WFH days on a pro-rata basis. 
 
ADDITIONAL WFH DAYS FOR EXISTING EMPLOYEES:  
 
For all existing employees, additional WFH days will be allocated annually based on the total months 
of service with AppX in the preceding year. 
1. Male Employees: 
o Regular Annual WFH Days: 12 days. 
o Additional WFH Days: Equal to the total months of service completed as of 31 st 
December each year. Example: An employee who completes 15 months of service by 
31st December 2024 will receive: 12 regular WFH days + 15 additional WFH days = 27 
WFH days for 2025. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
2. Female Employees: 
o Regular Annual WFH Days: 18 days. 
o Additional WFH Days: Equal to the total months of service completed as of 31st 
December 2024. Example: An employee who completes 15 months of service by 31st  
December 2024 will receive:  18 WFH + 15 additional WFH days = 33 WFH days for 
2025. 
 
APPLICATION PROCESS:  
• All WFH requests beyond 2 days should be submitted via GreytHR at least 2 weeks in advance 
for planned WFH.  
 
APPROVAL AND TRACKING:  
• Long-Term WFH: Requests for three or more consecutive WFH days are considered long-term 
WFH and must be discussed with and approved by the delivery lead, before raising the request 
in GreytHR. 
• Exceeding Limits: If employees exceed their annual WFH allocation, the additional days will be 
treated as half-day leave and recorded in the GreytHR system 
 
SPECIAL WORK FROM HOME (SWFH) : 
• Marriage: Employees are eligible for up to 3 weeks of SWFH, which can be utilized within one 
month before or after the wedding. 
• Childbirth:  
Paternity Benefit: Employees are entitled to 4 weeks of additional WFH, to be utilized within 
six months of the child’s birth. 
• Bereavement WFH: In the unfortunate event of the death of an immediate family member 
(parents, spouse, siblings, or children), employees will be granted up to 10 days of WFH to 
provide the necessary time to grieve and manage personal responsibilities. 
 
WFH WORK GUIDELINES:  
 
• Breaks: 
o A maximum of two breaks per day is allowed. 
o Evening breaks: 15 to 20 minutes maximum. 
o Dinner breaks: 30–40 minutes. 
o Additional breaks require prior approval from project stakeholders. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
• Connectivity and Environment: 
o Employees must ensure stable internet connectivity to maintain productivity. 
o A noise-free environment is required to avoid disruptions. 
• Meetings: 
o Timely attendance at all scheduled meetings is mandatory. 
o Breaks should be planned around meetings to ensure uninterrupted participation. 
 
ADDITIONAL NOTES  
• Employees in roles requiring on -site presence will be working as per project/ client 
requirements. 
• WFH is limited to 5 days over a period of 30 days, excluding special circumstances.  
• Maximum annual allocation of additional WFH days is capped at 60. 
• Bench resources are not eligible to avail WFH (excluding special circumstances) 
• Female employees not allocated to a project or working as interns are entitled to 1 WFH day 
per month. 
• Unused WFH/ SWFH cannot be carried forward to the following calendar year. 
 
CLIENT ESCALATION CLAUSE:  
 
In the event of client escalations due to unavailability, network issues, audio disruptions, or delayed 
responses while working remotely, the Delivery Managers reserves the right to revoke WFH privileges 
for the employees involved. 
This policy reflects our commitment to maintaining productivity and ensuring seamless operations 
while supporting flexible work arrangements.
