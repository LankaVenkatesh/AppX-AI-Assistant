 
 
 
 
Notice Period Policy 
 
Effective Date: January 1, 2025 
 
All employees of AppXcelerate Solutions Pvt Ltd  who choose to resign from their position are 
required to serve a 90-day notice period, commencing from the date of resignation. 
If an employee does not fulfill the complete notice period, they shall be required to compensate 
the Company with an amount equivalent to their salary for the unserved portion of the 90-day 
notice period. 
 
The Company reserves the right to: 
1. Adjust or waive the notice period based on business requirements at its sole discretion. 
2. Deny any request for early release if the employee's exit may impact ongoing projects or 
operations. 
 
Any modifications to the notice period must be approved in writing by the management. This 
policy applies uniformly to all employees unless otherwise specified in their employment 
contract.
