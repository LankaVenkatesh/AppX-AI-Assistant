 
Food Policy 
Date: 13 April 2026                                                                                                           Ref.No: HR/Admin/02/26-27 
 
1. Overview 
This policy outlines the guidelines for meal provisions, subscription costs, and collection procedures for all 
employees and interns at AppXcelerate Solutions Pvt Ltd. 
 
2. Eligibility and Pricing 
• New Joiners: Food is provided at free of cost during the initial joining calendar month. 
• Interns: Food is provided at free of cost for the duration of the internship. 
• Full-Time Employees: Meals are subsidized and charged at a flat rate of 750 per meal per month. 
➢ Lunch: 750 per month. 
➢ Dinner: 750 per month. 
➢ Both Meals: 1,500 per month. 
• Non-Prorated Charges: The full monthly amount will be deducted even if an employee consumes a 
meal for only one day. This is a monthly subscription, not a day-based system. 
 
3. Coupon Issuance and Collection 
Food coupons must be collected from the Admin Cabin during the designated window: 
• Opening Date: Available from the 27th of the month. 
➢ If the 27th is a holiday, coupons will be available on the next working day. 
➢ For the month of February, coupons are available from the 25th. 
• Closing Date: Coupon issuance closes on the 3rd of the subsequent month. 
➢ If the 3rd is a holiday, the window closes on the next working day. 
• Liability: The company is not responsible if an employee collects coupons but does not consume the 
food. The monthly amount will still be deducted. 
 
4. Opting Out or Changing Subscription 
• Deadline: To opt out for the following month, employees must send an official email to the Admin 
Team on or before the 25th of the current month. 
• Automatic Renewal: Failure to send an email by the 25 th will result in an automatic renewal for the 
next month. The deduction will occur even if the employee does not collect their coupons. 
• Official Channel: Requests must be sent strictly via email. WhatsApp messages to the Admin Team 
number or personal accounts will not be accepted. 
 
 
 
 
5. Official Meal Timings 
To ensure proper service, employees must adhere to the following timings: 
• Lunch: 12:45 PM to 2:00 PM. 
• Dinner: 7:45 PM to 10:15 PM. 
 
6. Reimbursement for Unavailability 
• If food is unavailable in the office during the official timings, employees should inform the Admin 
Team immediately. 
• Employees may then order food externally and claim reimbursement through the GreytHR portal.
