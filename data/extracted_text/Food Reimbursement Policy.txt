 
Food Reimbursement Policy 
Ref. number: HR/Admin/01/26-27 (Revised)                                                                  Date: 13 April 2026  
 
1. Policy Overview 
This policy defines the guidelines for the reimbursement of food expenses incurred by employees 
while performing official duties. 
2. Reimbursement Limits & Timing (Effective FY 2026-27) 
• Employees are eligible for reimbursement of up to Rs. 175 per meal  or the actual amount 
incurred, whichever is lower. 
• Official Timings: Reimbursement is provided only if the food is consumed during the official food 
timings mentioned as per the Food Policy. 
• Hours Restriction:  Meals consumed before or after  the official hours are not entitled to 
reimbursement. 
• Processing Schedule: Approved reimbursements will be processed between the 6th to 10th of 
the subsequent month. Please note that these payments will be processed independently and 
not along with the regular payroll. 
3. Eligibility Criteria 
• Reimbursement applies strictly to meals consumed for official work purposes. 
• This policy applies only to employees who have opted for lunch and/or dinner provisions 
through the company. 
• If food is unavailable in the office, employees may claim reimbursement provided they inform 
the Admin team in advance or at the time of the requirement. 
4. Documentation Requirements 
• A valid tax invoice or bill must be uploaded for every claim. 
• Payment screenshots (e.g., UPI or card transactions) will not be accepted as proof of expense. 
• Reimbursements will be accepted only for online food orders supported by a valid invoice or 
order history. 
5. Claim Submission Process 
• All claims must be submitted through the GreytHR portal. 
• Claims submitted without proper documentation or outside the system will not be processed. 
6. Compliance & Exceptions 
• Any exceptions to this policy require prior approval from the Reporting Manager or HR. 
• The company reserves the right to reject claims that do not comply with these guidelines.
