 
 
  
 
APPX LEAVE AND ENCASHMENT POLICY  
 
Effective Date: 1 Jan 2025 
Last Updated: 30 Dec 2024 
 
OBJECTIVE  
 
The purpose of this policy is to outline the guidelines for leave entitlements, leave encashment, and 
compensatory offs for employees of AppXcelerate Solutions Pvt Ltd ("AppX"). This policy ensures 
transparency, compliance, and fairness in leave management. 
 
ANNUAL LEAVE ALLOCATION  
 
1.1 Earned Leaves (EL): 
• Employees are entitled to 16 Earned Leaves annually. 
• Earned Leaves can be used for personal reasons, vacations, or other planned absences. 
 
1.2 Sick Leaves (SL): 
• Employees are entitled to 6 Sick Leaves annually. 
• Sick Leaves are to be utilized for medical reasons and may require a medical certificate for 
absences exceeding two consecutive days. 
 
LEAVE ENCASHMENT  
 
2.1 Eligibility for Leave Encashment: 
• Employees working from the office are eligible for earned leave encashment based on their 
last drawn Gross CTC. 
• For employees working from home or in a hybrid model, unused earned leave encashment 
will be calculated based on their basic salary. 
 
2.2 Encashment Guidelines: 
• Earned Leaves will be encashed at the end of the calendar year. 
• The encashment amount will be calculated based on the employee’s last drawn gross salary. 
• Sick Leaves are not eligible for encashment  and cannot be carried forward  to the following 
year. 
 
COMPENSATORY OFF  
 
3.1 Eligibility for Comp Off: 
• Employees who work on a company -declared holiday or weekend with prior client approval 
are eligible for a Comp Off. 
• The Comp Off request must be supported by the timesheet reflecting client approval for the 
day worked. 
 
 
 
 
 
3.2 Usage of Comp Off: 
• Comp Off must be availed within 90 days of being credited. 
• If the Comp Off validity extends into the next year, it will be carried forward. 
• Comp Offs are not eligible for encashments. 
 
GENERAL GUIDELINES  
• All leave requests must be submitted and approved in advance via the company’s HR portal, 
except in the case of emergencies or Sick Leaves. 
• Misuse of leaves or failure to comply with leave policies may result in disciplinary action. 
• The company reserves the right to amend this policy at its discretion.
